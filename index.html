The FizzBuzz Game

The rules are simple:

For every number divisible by 3 you have to say Fizz

For every number divisible by 5 you have to say Buzz

For every number divisible by both 3 and 5 you have to say FizzBuzz

You have 60 seconds to get as many as you can right. But beware!

If you mess it up you will lose all points!
